# Moments App - 3-Minute Demo Script

## Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)

**"Hi, I'm going to show you Moments - a beautiful life journaling app that helps you capture your daily experiences and turn them into meaningful stories."**

**Key Points:**
- Mobile-first design with a beautiful, phone-like interface
- Secure data storage with Skyflow encryption
- AI-powered daily summaries
- Calendar view for tracking mood over time

---

## Feature 1: Calendar View (0:30 - 1:00)

**"Let's start with the calendar view - this gives you a bird's eye view of your month."**

**Actions:**
1. Click the calendar icon in the top right
2. Show the month view with mood indicators
3. Toggle to week view
4. Click on a day with a summary to show the dashboard

**Talking Points:**
- "Each day is color-coded by mood - green for great days, amber for okay days, red for challenging days"
- "You can switch between month and week views"
- "Days with summaries show emoji indicators"
- "The AI analyzes your patterns and shows detected themes"

---

## Feature 2: Adding Moments (1:00 - 1:30)

**"Now let's capture a moment. This is where the magic happens."**

**Actions:**
1. Navigate back to the input view
2. Type a moment: "Just finished a great run in Golden Gate Park"
3. Show the moment appearing in the timeline
4. Point out the auto-detected emotion and tags
5. Show the Strava integration badge if applicable

**Talking Points:**
- "Simply type what's happening - the app automatically detects your emotion and tags"
- "Moments are stored locally for privacy - your thoughts stay on your device"
- "If you have Strava connected, fitness activities automatically sync"
- "Each moment shows location, weather, and timestamp"

---

## Feature 3: Creating Daily Story (1:30 - 2:00)

**"After capturing a few moments, you can generate your daily story."**

**Actions:**
1. Show multiple moments in the timeline
2. Click "Create My Day's Story" button
3. Show the processing animation
4. Navigate to the dashboard view showing the generated story

**Talking Points:**
- "The AI weaves your moments into a personalized narrative"
- "You get highlights, recommended actions, and mood tracking"
- "Each highlight is traceable back to the original moment"
- "The story is stored locally - your private thoughts stay private"

---

## Feature 4: Skyflow Profile Integration (2:00 - 2:45)

**"Now let's look at the secure profile storage powered by Skyflow."**

**Actions:**
1. Click the profile menu (HM avatar)
2. Click "Profile"
3. Show the profile page
4. Point out: Name (Henry Mai), Date of Birth (01-01-1995), Nationality (Vietnam)
5. Show the Skyflow ID

**Talking Points:**
- "User profile data is securely stored in Skyflow with tokenization"
- "Only profile information goes to Skyflow - moments stay local"
- "You can see the Skyflow ID for this record"
- "Data is encrypted and tokenized for maximum security"
- "The profile shows: Name, Date of Birth formatted as DD-MM-YYYY, and Nationality"

---

## Feature 5: Strava Integration (2:45 - 3:00)

**"One more thing - if you have Strava connected, fitness activities automatically appear as moments."**

**Actions:**
1. Navigate back to moments view
2. Point out a moment with Strava activity badge
3. Show the activity stats (distance, duration, calories)

**Talking Points:**
- "Runs, walks, hikes, and bike rides automatically sync"
- "Activity stats are displayed with each moment"
- "This creates a complete picture of your day - both mental and physical"

---

## Closing (3:00)

**"That's Moments - a beautiful way to capture your life, powered by secure Skyflow storage and AI insights. Thank you!"**

---

## Key Demo Tips:

1. **Keep it flowing** - Don't pause too long between actions
2. **Highlight security** - Emphasize Skyflow encryption and local storage for moments
3. **Show the UI polish** - Point out the beautiful animations and design
4. **Emphasize privacy** - Moments stay local, only profile data goes to Skyflow
5. **Be enthusiastic** - The app is visually appealing, show that energy!

---

## Quick Reference - Feature Highlights:

✅ **Calendar View** - Visual mood tracking over time
✅ **Moment Capture** - Quick, distraction-free entry
✅ **AI Summaries** - Personalized daily stories
✅ **Skyflow Security** - Encrypted profile storage
✅ **Strava Integration** - Automatic fitness activity sync
✅ **Privacy First** - Moments stored locally, not in Skyflow
